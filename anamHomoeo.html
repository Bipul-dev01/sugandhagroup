About Anam Homoeo

Anam Homoeo is a trusted and renowned sister concern of Sugandha Group, dedicated to providing quality homoeopathic healthcare solutions. Under the leadership of its esteemed Managing Director, A. H. Jihad (Dr. Anam), the clinic has become a symbol of hope and healing for countless patients across the country.

Dr. Anam is a highly respected and experienced homoeopathic physician, recognized for his expertise and commitment to patient care. His academic qualifications include:

  • DHMS (Reg: 37871, Serial: 11638)
  • BSS (Hon's)
  • MSS in Political Science from the University of Dhaka

With a strong foundation in both homoeopathic medicine and academic excellence, Dr. Anam has successfully treated a wide range of chronic and complex diseases. His areas of specialization include:

  • Cancer
  • Skin Diseases
  • Impotency
  • Infertility
  • And many other critical health conditions

Anam Homoeo uses high-quality medicines sourced directly from Germany, especially from the reputed BT Company, ensuring effective and safe treatment outcomes for patients.

Thousands of patients have received treatment from Dr. Anam and experienced remarkable improvement in their health. Many have found hope after struggling with conditions previously considered untreatable, making Anam Homoeo a trusted destination for natural healing.

Driven by compassion, knowledge, and dedication, Anam Homoeo continues to deliver holistic and natural healing through modern homoeopathic practices. The clinic welcomes patients from all walks of life, offering personalized treatment plans and expert care in a professional and friendly environment.
